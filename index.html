An Epic Idle Adventure

🎮 Welcome, Adventurer!

Welcome to Grindia, an idle RPG where you master skills, craft powerful equipment, and battle fearsome monsters. Progress through gathering, production, and combat as you build your legacy in this epic adventure!

🚀 Quick Start Guide

1️⃣

Choose Your First Skill

Start with Woodcutting, Mining, or Fishing to gather resources. Click a skill in the sidebar to begin!

2️⃣

Gain XP & Level Up

Each action grants XP. Level up to unlock new resources and activities. Don't forget about Mastery - it reduces action time!

3️⃣

Craft Equipment

Mine ores and use Blacksmithing to smelt bars and craft powerful weapons and armor. Better gear = stronger combat stats!

4️⃣

Enter Combat

Fight monsters in Combat zones! Cook food to heal, equip your best gear, and earn valuable loot. Important: Food is essential - combat is balanced!

5️⃣

Progress Further

Complete Contracts for rewards, spend Mastery Points on upgrades, and hunt for rare Trinkets to boost your skills!

💡 Pro Tips

⚔️
Combat Balance

After recent changes, food is now important! Always keep cooked food ready for tough fights.

⭐
Master Everything

Mastery reduces action time by up to 50%! Each resource has its own mastery track.

🏪
Bank Management

Your bank auto-saves! Sell junk items for gold or use the category filters to organize.

🔨
Equipment Sets

Higher tier equipment requires higher combat stats. Plan your progression carefully!

ℹ️ How to Earn Mastery Points

Gain mastery levels by performing actions repeatedly:

  • Gathering/Production skills: Each action has separate mastery (8 per skill)
  • Combat: Each monster has separate mastery (28 total monsters)

Points awarded: 2.5 points per mastery level (rounded down)

Example: Level 99 mastery = 247 points from that action/monster
